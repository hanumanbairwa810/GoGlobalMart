Cookies Policy

How we use cookies to improve your experience

Last Updated: January 2025
Effective Date: January 15, 2025
1

Introduction

GoGlobalMart ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to providing you with the best possible shopping experience. As part of that commitment, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance functionality, analyze usage patterns, and deliver personalized content.

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit our website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them each time you visit or navigate between pages.

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, the types of cookies we deploy, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. By continuing to use GoGlobalMart, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

2

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of purposes to ensure that our website functions smoothly and provides a relevant, personalized experience for every visitor. Below are the primary ways in which cookies are utilized on GoGlobalMart:

  • Improve Website Performance Cookies help us identify which pages and features are performing well and which ones need improvement. This allows us to optimize load times, fix errors, and deliver a faster, smoother browsing experience.
  • Remember User Preferences We use cookies to store your language preferences, currency settings, font size choices, and other customization options so that your experience remains consistent across sessions.
  • Analyze Traffic & Behavior Cookies collect anonymized data about how visitors interact with our site — including pages visited, time spent, click patterns, and navigation paths — which helps us make data-driven decisions to improve content and layout.
3

Types of Cookies

We use three main categories of cookies on GoGlobalMart. Each serves a distinct purpose, and some are necessary for the website to function properly while others enhance your experience or support our affiliate partnerships.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of our website. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, secure access to authenticated areas, and shopping cart management. Essential cookies cannot be disabled, as the site will not function properly without them.

Required — Cannot be disabled

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. These cookies allow us to measure the number of visitors, identify popular pages, and detect performance issues — all of which help us continuously improve the user experience.

Optional — Can be disabled

Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our ad campaigns. As an affiliate e-commerce platform, these cookies also help us attribute purchases to the correct affiliate partners and ensure you see deals and products that match your interests.

Optional — Can be disabled

5

Managing Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. Most web browsers provide options to block or delete cookies, limit third-party cookies, or set preferences for specific websites.

To manage cookies in your browser, follow these general steps:

  • Google Chrome Go to Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data. From here you can block third-party cookies, clear existing cookies, or add site-specific exceptions.
  • Mozilla Firefox Navigate to Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data. You can choose your preferred level of cookie blocking and manage exceptions.
  • Apple Safari Go to Preferences → Privacy. Here you can block all cookies, prevent cross-site tracking, or manage stored website data.
  • Microsoft Edge Open Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies and site data. You can block or allow cookies as needed.

Important: Disabling or blocking certain cookies — especially essential cookies — may affect the functionality of our website. Some features such as the shopping cart, user login, and personalized recommendations may not work as intended if cookies are restricted.
